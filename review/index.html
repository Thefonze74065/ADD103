Hollow Knight: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong is a 2D action-adventure metroidvania game developed by Team Cherry.
After 7 years of development, It was released in September 2025 following the success of its predecessor, Hollow Knight.

Gameplay and Mechanics

Silksong builds upon the mechanics of Hollow Knight, Introducing new abilities and combat styles in the forms of crests and tools.
The game also brings back a variety of existing mechanics, such as the ability to dash, wall jump, and double jump.
At its core, Silksong has the same gameplay loop as Hollow Knight, which is to explore a dangerous world, and defeat challenging bosses while uncovering the story of the game.

Sherma

Official Description: An optimistic pilgrim on a spiritual journey. Sherma’s path may often cross with Hornet’s own, but his lack of combat and acrobatic prowess can land him in desperate need of aid.

Sherma is a friendly NPC who the player meets early in the game. He is a pilgrim who is on a journey to reach the top of the game world. He seemingly has no function other than to act cute and bond with the player.
In the previous game, a similar character called Myla was killed during the game's events, leading most players to dread the same fate for Sherma.

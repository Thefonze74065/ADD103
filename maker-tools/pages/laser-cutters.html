Laser Cutters

Laser cutters use a focused beam of light to cut, engrave, or etch materials with precision. They’re fast, accurate, and can handle everything from paper to acrylics.

Types of Laser Cutters

  • CO₂ Lasers: Great for wood, acrylic, and leather.
  • Fiber Lasers: Best for metals like steel or aluminum.
  • Diode Lasers: Affordable and compact, ideal for hobbyists.

What Can You Make?

  • Signage & Displays: Crisp lettering and logos.
  • Custom Gifts: Engraved cutting boards, jewelry, or glassware.
  • Prototyping & Fabrication: Cut parts that fit together with high precision.

Why Use It?

Laser cutters are versatile and perfect for both detailed artwork and practical parts.

CNC Mills

CNC (Computer Numerical Control) mills are machines that carve solid materials like wood, plastic, or metal into precise shapes. Guided by computer code, they make repeatable and accurate parts.

Types of CNC Mills

  • 3-Axis Mills: Move in three directions, suitable for most projects.
  • 4- and 5-Axis Mills: Allow more complex cuts and sculpted surfaces.
  • Desktop CNCs: Compact and affordable, great for small workshops.

What Can You Make?

  • Mechanical Parts: Gears, brackets, and machine components.
  • Furniture & Woodwork: Custom tables, signs, and cabinetry.
  • Metalwork: Durable and precise components for engineering.

Why Use It?

CNC mills are essential for precision, repeatability, and professional-quality results in both prototyping and production.
